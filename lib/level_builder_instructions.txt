usage:

builde.py filename w h

if file exist it will be loaded for editing (files are save in data/levels


1-8 tiles, 7 is exit tile, 9 coins, 0 circle

asdw  move level around

q/e rotate selected tile

mouse: 

click on tile to select and move around


capslock enables/disables grid snapping (grid can be changed by mouse wheel)

b moves the ball to the mouse pisition

delete removes selected tile

o saves the level
p saves and start it
